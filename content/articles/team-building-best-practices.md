## Psychological Safety

Create an environment where people can raise issues without fear. Blameless post-mortems help teams learn from incidents.

## Clear Goals

Define clear outcomes and let the team decide how to reach them. Autonomy with direction drives ownership.

## Regular Feedback

Provide timely, constructive feedback. Retrospectives and one-on-ones build understanding and alignment.
